# Telecom-Customer-Churn-Analysis
The goal is to help businesses understand churn drivers and improve customer retention through data-driven insights.
## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Customer Churn Analysis using Power BI. The dashboard analyzes customer behavior, service subscriptions, billing patterns, and contract types to identify key factors influencing customer churn.

The goal is to help businesses understand churn drivers and improve customer retention through data-driven insights.

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains telecom customer information, including:

- Customer demographics (gender, senior citizen status, partner)
- Account information (tenure, contract type)
- Services subscribed (Internet, StreamingTV, StreamingMovies, PhoneService, OnlineSecurity)
- Billing details (MonthlyCharges, TotalCharges, PaymentMethod, PaperlessBilling)
- Target variable: Churn (Yes/No)

Total customers analyzed: ~7,000

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Customer Demographic Analysis

Insights include:

- Customer distribution by gender
- Churn rate by tenure
- Monthly charges trend across tenure
- Senior citizen churn comparison
- Customer count by tenure group

Key insight:
Customers with lower tenure have significantly higher churn rates.

---

### 2. Service Subscription Analysis

Insights include:

- Churn by Internet Service (Fiber optic, DSL, No internet)
- Churn by StreamingTV and StreamingMovies
- Total customers by Internet Service
- Key performance indicators:
  - Average tenure
  - Average monthly charges
  - Total customers
  - Average total charges

Key insight:
Fiber optic customers show higher churn rates compared to DSL.

---

### 3. Contract & Billing Insights

Insights include:

- Churn by contract type (Month-to-month, One year, Two year)
- Churn by payment method
- Monthly charges by payment method
- Phone service impact on churn

Key insight:
Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.

---

### 4. Churn Analysis

Insights include:

- Overall churn distribution
- Churn rate by contract type
- Churn rate by payment method
- Churn rate by OnlineSecurity

Key insight:
Customers without online security are more likely to churn.

---

### 5. Key Business Analysis

Advanced insights include:

- Churn rate by tenure group
- Churn rate by paperless billing
- Churn risk segmentation (Low, Medium, High)
- Revenue retained vs revenue lost due to churn

Business impact:

Revenue retained: 13.19M  
Revenue lost due to churn: 2.86M  
Overall churn rate: 27%

---
